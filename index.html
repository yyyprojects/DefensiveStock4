The Anatomy of a Defensive Stock

Three key metrics define their stable nature.

Low Beta

< 1.0

Beta measures volatility against the market. A beta under 1.0 means the stock is less volatile, moving less dramatically than the market as a whole.

Where to Find Defensive Stocks

They are concentrated in sectors that provide the necessities of modern life.

Consumer Staples

Companies providing essential everyday goods like food, drinks, and household products.

Examples: Procter & Gamble, Coca-Cola, Tyson Foods

Utilities

Firms supplying vital services like electricity, water, and natural gas that power our homes and businesses.

Examples: Duke Energy, NextEra Energy

Healthcare

Businesses in pharmaceuticals, medical equipment, and healthcare services that are always in demand.

Examples: Johnson & Johnson, Pfizer, UnitedHealth
